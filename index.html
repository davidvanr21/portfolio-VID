Who I Am

I'm a young digital designer who loves to make perfect user-friendly products.

It doesn't matter if you're on the go, chilling in a hottub, running a maranthon or flying in a private jet, products need to work as you expect. My personal goal is to give the perfect experience of products for all people. Everywhere and anytime.

Visual focused

From a young age I was making websites and films. I've always been busy (and a little bit in love) with the visual side of projects. I love making sketches as much as possible, to bring different variaties of an idea in a project to develope the best outcome.

A little bit nerdy...

I think it's key to have the knowledge of coding to discuss things with developers. With that reason, I'm a little nerdy myself and I love to dive into some coding once in a while. I have much experience with HTML & CSS and also understand the JavaScript language.
