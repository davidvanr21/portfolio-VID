Menu

An easy to use tutorial for the FIFA controls on PS4.

A project where we could experiment with animation and motion. Because it is so important in multimedia interfaces nowadays, it's also very important to understand the principles as a designer. And what kind of animmation is important to a certain context.

This project has been a great example of good iteration and adjustments to my first designs. Watch my process of design here on milanote or download a PDF here.

Because my first design was not finished yet, I could still make a lot of changes. It wasn't already programmed or something like that, so I first went back to brainstorm to come up some ideas. I worked this all out inside of Milanote which was a great tool to use for documenting my process.

Final Result

I'm super super happy with the end result. I think the iteration and improvements on first version are extremely well done. I listened to the feedback of students and my coach and took the prototyping to another level with my coding skills. It was a very challenging experiment, but the prototype works fast and good.
